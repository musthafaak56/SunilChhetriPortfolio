Sunil Chhetri
Sunil Chhetri is a professional soccer player from India. He currently serves as the captain of both the Indian national team and the Indian Super League team Bengaluru FC. Chhetri is widely regarded as one of the best soccer players in the history of Indian football, and he is the most-capped player and all-time top scorer for the Indian national team.

Chhetri began his professional career in 2002, signing with Mohun Bagan AC of the Indian National Football League. He has also played for clubs in the United States, Portugal, and the United Kingdom, including Major League Soccer team Sporting Kansas City and English Football League team Queens Park Rangers. In 2017, Chhetri returned to India to join Bengaluru FC, where he has been a key player and captain.

In addition to his club career, Chhetri has had a successful international career with the Indian national team. He made his debut for the team in 2005 and has since scored over 70 goals in international competition. Chhetri has represented India in the AFC Asian Cup and the SAFF Championship, and he played a key role in helping the team qualify for the AFC Asian Cup for the first time in 27 years.
